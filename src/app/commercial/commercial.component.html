Commercial GEOfence Marketing ADVERTISE TO YOUR TARGET DEMOGRAPHIC WITH MOBILE AD DISPLAY

In the race to increase your agency’s ROI, you need tools to precisely target your prospective customers and give yourself a major competitive edge over other marketing models. Commercial GEOFence Marketing gives you the tools you need to hit your customers with specific, targeted advertising right on their phones or other mobile devices.

Let’s break down how GEOFence marketing can help you!

The mobile device market is the fastest-growing market in the digital display space. GEOfencing allows advertisers to throw up a virtual fence around an area and then send targeted advertising to any device that has crossed that fence line. The applications are numerous, including:

 Fence events like trade shows to target customers who’ve shown interest in products and services similar to what your customers are offering. Fence competitors and send ads to customers already engaged in the shopping process and offer them better deals. Fence individual buildings and households in your vicinity and drive them to your business.
