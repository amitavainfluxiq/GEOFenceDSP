How Big Data works

There are almost countless ways for companies to harvest Big Data. Information can be gathered from online forms and surveys, social media sites, and many other sources. The companies then segment this data according to different criteria and store it.

Depending on how the data was gathered, collectors and aggregators can glean very specific information from the data they take in. Those insights can then be used to target very specific populations of customers, sending them targeted display ads that pop up in apps they are using on their phones.
